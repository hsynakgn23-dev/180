Privacy Policy

This policy explains how 180 Absolute Cinema collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the web application, the mobile application, and related services.

Who This Applies To

This policy applies to users of 180 Absolute Cinema across the website, mobile app, account system, analytics pipeline, referral features, and push notification features.

What We Collect

Account And Profile Data

We may collect your email address, password-based login credentials through our authentication provider, full name, username, gender, birth date, avatar, and profile bio.

User Content

We collect the comments, rituals, replies, reactions, follows, invite codes, and other content you create or submit inside the product.

Device And Usage Data

We may collect session identifiers, page paths, referrer data, attribution parameters, app events, local storage identifiers, and a device-level referral key used to reduce abuse.

Notifications

If mobile notifications are enabled, we may collect push permission status, push token, and limited notification delivery metadata needed to send and manage notifications.

How We Use Data

  • To create and secure accounts, authenticate users, and maintain sessions.
  • To display profiles, comments, rituals, follows, and other social features.
  • To sync data between local storage, the website, and mobile experiences.
  • To operate referrals, invite claims, abuse prevention, and rate limiting.
  • To deliver notifications when you opt in on mobile.
  • To measure product usage, diagnose failures, and improve reliability.

Legal Basis And Choices

We use your data to perform the service you request, maintain product security, and improve the application. You can update profile fields from inside the app and you can stop using the service at any time. You can also disable notifications from your device settings.

Storage And Retention

  • Some data is stored locally on your device or browser, including session state, cached experience state, and preference data.
  • Account, profile, content, and sync records may also be stored in our backend systems and service providers.
  • We retain data for as long as it is reasonably needed to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and prevent abuse.

Sharing And Service Providers

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with infrastructure and service providers that help us operate the product, including services for authentication, backend storage, push notifications, analytics, and sign-in flows.

Cookies And Local Storage

The web application uses browser storage and similar technical mechanisms to keep you signed in, store app state, remember preferences, preserve analytics session context, and support referral and deep link flows.

Your Rights

  • You may request access to the personal data associated with your account.
  • You may request correction of inaccurate profile data.
  • You may request deletion of your account data, subject to legal or security requirements. Use the published Account Deletion instructions.
  • You may revoke push notification permission from your device settings.

Children

180 Absolute Cinema is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the service, please contact the operator through the public channels linked on the website so the information can be reviewed and removed where appropriate.

International Transfers

Because our service providers and infrastructure may operate in multiple countries, your data may be processed outside your country of residence.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this policy when the product, legal requirements, or service providers change. The latest version will be published on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact the 180 Absolute Cinema operator through the public contact channels linked in the product footer or the support channel listed in the app store listing for the application.

For account removal specifically, use the dedicated Account Deletion page for the current request path and retention notes.
